Shipping & Delivery Policy

Transparent logistics   •   Reliable timelines   •   OTP confirmation

📋 SHIPPING FRAMEWORK

Drapso facilitates order processing between wholesalers and resellers. Wholesellers are responsible for packing and shipping the products to customers.

Estimated delivery timelines may vary depending on the product, location, and logistics partner. Typically, orders are delivered within 3–10 business days.

Delivery is considered complete only after OTP-based confirmation from the customer. This confirmation is mandatory for payout processing.

In case of delays due to unforeseen circumstances such as logistics issues, weather conditions, or other external factors, Drapso will not be held liable but will assist in resolving the issue.

Shipping charges, if applicable, are included in the final order price or displayed at checkout.

✅ Trust & transparency: OTP confirmation ensures safe delivery & seamless payouts for partners.
