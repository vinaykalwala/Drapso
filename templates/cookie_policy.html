Cookie Policy – Drapso

Drapso uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, ensure platform functionality, and improve overall service performance. By accessing or using Drapso, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

Cookies are used to support essential platform operations such as account authentication, session management, and secure access to user dashboards for wholesalers and resellers. These technologies help ensure that users can log in securely, maintain active sessions, and access personalized features without interruption.

We also use cookies to understand how users interact with the platform, including navigation behavior, feature usage, and general engagement. This information helps us improve platform performance, optimize user experience, and develop new features.

Cookies may also be used to support security measures, including fraud detection, prevention of unauthorized access, and protection of user accounts and transactions. This is especially important for maintaining the integrity of payment processing and wallet systems on the platform.

  • Session management & secure authentication for wholesalers and resellers
  • Analytics to improve platform performance & feature development
  • Fraud prevention & payment integrity protection

In certain cases, cookies may be used to remember user preferences such as language settings, device preferences, and other customization options to provide a more personalized experience.

Third-Party Services: Drapso may allow third-party service providers, including payment gateways and analytics providers, to use cookies or similar technologies as required to deliver their services. These third parties operate under their own privacy and data protection policies.

Users have the option to control or disable cookies through their browser or device settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on the platform, including login sessions, store management, and payment-related operations.

Drapso reserves the right to update or modify this Cookie Policy at any time. Continued use of the platform after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
