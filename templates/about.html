About Drapso

Drapso is a next-generation social commerce platform designed to simplify online selling for everyone. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to start and grow their online stores without the need for inventory, large investments, or technical expertise.

Collaboration

We connect wholesalers and resellers on a single platform, enabling seamless collaboration. Wholesellers can list their products and reach a wide network of resellers, while resellers can instantly create their own online stores, add products with one click, and start selling without managing stock or logistics.

Complex Parts Handled

At Drapso, we handle the complex parts of eCommerce — including payment processing, order flow, and payout distribution — so that our users can focus on what matters most: selling and growing their business.

Transparency and Trust

Our platform is built on transparency and trust. All payments are securely processed, and earnings are distributed only after successful delivery verification. We ensure a fair and structured system where wholesalers, resellers, and the platform all benefit from each transaction.



No Barriers. Just Success.

We believe that anyone should be able to start an online business without barriers. Whether you are a student, a small business owner, or an aspiring entrepreneur, Drapso provides the tools you need to succeed in the digital marketplace.
